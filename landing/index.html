Features

Everything a Muslim
Parent Needs

Designed to fit into the rhythm of your day — not add to your to-do list.

📖
Daily Insights
One spiritual insight and one research-backed tip, freshly generated every morning. Each comes with a practical action step you can try today.
🧠
Personalized Learning
Go deeper on any parenting topic. Build a multi-lesson module on subjects like emotional regulation, Islamic discipline, or managing screen time — tailored to your family.
🎯
Family Goals
Set goals your family works toward together — praying in congregation, reading Quran, masjid visits, and more. Gentle reminders keep each goal on track without the pressure.

Personalized to Your Family
Set your children's ages and your parenting focus areas — from Quran & Prayer to Emotional Regulation, Screen Time, Special Needs, and more. Your insights stay relevant as your family grows.
🏡
Sync with Your Family
Parenting is a team effort. Share your family goals with your co-parent so everyone stays aligned — same values, same approach.
📊
Progress Tracking
Track your reading streaks, goal check-ins, and weekly consistency. See your month at a glance and build the habit one day at a time.
🔖
Personal Library
Bookmark any insight to revisit later. Build a personal reference of the guidance that resonated most, synced across all your devices.
🔗
Cited Sources
Every insight links back to its source — a lecture, research paper, or article — so you can always go deeper on what resonates.
